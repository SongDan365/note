model          n.    型号，样式
afford         v.    付得起（钱）
deposit        n.    预付定金
instalment     n.    分期付款
price          n.    价格
millionaire    n.    百万富翁

---

1. 两个音节以上的形容词比较级用 more/less，最高级用 most/least。
   例子：This model's less expensive than that one. The other models's more expensive. It's the most expensive model in the shop.

2. it's not as good as the expensive one，not as ... as ... 可以用来比较，意思是前面的不如后面的。

3. buy...on installments, 以分期付款的方式购买。
